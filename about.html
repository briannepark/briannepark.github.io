For the Love of Art

Brianne Park, more recognizably referred to as Brikrap, is a Hawai'i-born and raised artist and engineer, who never stays in none place for long. Brikrap began drawing and at the age of 5, with a love for ocean animals and scenery. As she got older, she lost gained interest in digital art + design, before giving up it all up almost completely for 5 years. It wasn't until she was inspired by decorating her home that she was drawn back to first love... art.


Brikrap's art has changed throughout the years, but has found a new-found love for textures. Her work is inspired by her life, whether it's her people, her environment, or her body.
